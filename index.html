Welcome to the UC Merced Air Quality Dashboard

Explore the interactive map, and click on datapoints to learn more about the data and sensors!

The tabs under the map can be utilized to switch between the air quality map, and the city level wind speed grid.

The tabs under the indicators and list can be utilized to switch between open-source data, and data from sensors installed by the Cobian Fire Lab.

Navigate to the sidebar on the left for an more information about the dashboard.
